Brownies

This is my favourite recipe

List of Ingredients

  • flour
  • nutella
  • eggs

Steps:

  1. Put flour inside a bowl
  2. Crack 3 eggs
  3. Pour 250g of nutella in the bowl
  4. Mix it
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degree celcius for 15minutes
  7. Enjoy!
